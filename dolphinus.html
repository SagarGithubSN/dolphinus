Our Vision

Dolphinus Sports Academy is a preschool-focused sports development initiative, introducing children (ages 3-10) to the fundamentals of sports in a fun, engaging, and educational environment. By harnessing the power of play-based learning, hands-on experiences, and individualized attention, we help each child discover their unique strengths and grow into confident, compassionate learners.

We believe in building a strong foundation for lifelong learning, ensuring children are physically active, socially well-adjusted, and emotionally resilient. Our programs encourage teamwork, coordination, self-confidence, and a love for physical activities.

Long-Term Athlete Development (LTAD)

We follow Active for Life principles, ensuring children progress through these stages:

  • Active Start (0-6 years)

    Building basic motor skills through fun, play-based activities.

  • Learn to Train

    Introducing fundamental sports skills and nurturing teamwork.

  • Train to Train

    Developing technique, agility, and game awareness.

  • Train to Compete

    Fostering competitive spirit and refining specialized skills.

  • Train to Win

    Preparing children for higher-level competitive play (if they choose).

Our goals include reducing screen dependency, promoting lifelong values, and addressing modern lifestyle challenges—ultimately empowering communities through an active lifestyle.

How We Train

Customized sports day events and specialized camps that dive deeper into specific sports—perfect for children seeking a more focused experience.

Certified instructors experienced in working with young children, ensuring a safe and encouraging environment.

Focus on coordination, spatial awareness, strength, agility, teamwork, and focus—helping children excel and prepare for higher challenges.
Special Training Methods

We implement The Littleace Method, making training fun and engaging for young children. Activities like Frog and Lily and Dribblers & Robbers improve cardio, limb strength, and ball-handling skills. Our technology-driven curriculum ensures children receive the best guidance possible.

Admissions

We’d love to welcome your child to the Dolphinus family. Our admissions process is designed to be smooth and parent-friendly:

  1. Fill out the enrollment form online or at our front desk.
  2. Schedule a tour to see our classrooms and meet our coaches.
  3. Discuss any unique needs or preferences with our staff.
  4. Finalize paperwork and confirm your child’s start date.

Our dedicated admissions team will guide you every step of the way, ensuring a positive experience for you and your child.
